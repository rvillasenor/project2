Beauty of Colorado
Colorado is one of the most beautiful states in all of America. With the mountains towering over the cities and the colorful skies lighting up the cities.
  • Colorado invented the cheeseburger
  • The world's first rodeo was held on July 4th, 1869 in Deer Trail
  • We have the world's largest flat-top mountain
  • Rocky Ford is the "melon capital of the world."
  • The highest suspension bridge in the world is over the Royal Gorge
